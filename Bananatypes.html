Different Types of Bananas

Cavendish Banana

The Cavendish banana is the most common type of banana found in grocery stores
worldwide. It has a sweet flavor and a creamy texture when ripe.

Red Banana

Red bananas have a reddish-purple skin and a sweeter taste compared to yellow bananas.
They are rich in vitamin C and beta-carotene.

Lady Finger Banana

Lady Finger bananas are smaller and sweeter than the Cavendish variety. They are often used
in desserts and fruit salads.

Blue Java Banana

Also known as the "ice cream banana," Blue Java bananas have a unique blue-tinged skin and a creamy
texture that resembles vanilla ice cream.

Burro Banana

Burro bananas are shorter and more square-shaped than other varieties. They have a
tangy flavor and are often used in cooking.
