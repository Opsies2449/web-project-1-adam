What are bananas?

Bananas are a popular yellow fruit known for their sweet taste and nutritional values.
They are rich in potassium, vitamin C, and dietary fiber.

Benefits of Bananas

  • Rich in important nutrients
  • Helps for digestion
  • Makes the heart healthy
  • Provides energy
  • Yummy for your tummy

Fun Facts about Bananas

  1. Bananas are technically berries.
  2. They float in water because they are less dense than water.
  3. There are over 1,000 different varieties of bananas across the world.

This is the first type of bananas before humans

This type of banana is known as the "wild banana" and is the ancestor of all the normal bananas we eat today.
Wild bananas are smaller, have more seeds and not as sweet as the other types.

When was bananas popularized?

Bananas were popularized in the late 19th and early 20th centuries, mostly in countries like the United States and Europe.
It is possible because of the introduction of refrigerated shipping that allowed bananas to be transported over long distances
without spoiling or rotting, making them more fresh and more accessible. Companies like the United Fruit Company played
a significant role in promoting bananas.
